How a Restaurant Group Cut Procurement Time by 70%

A multi-location restaurant group received dozens of supplier price quotes through WhatsApp every week. Comparing prices manually across suppliers was slow, messy, and error-prone.

The solution

Automated WhatsApp quote extraction and live price comparison

We built a system that automatically extracts quotes from WhatsApp messages, organizes them by product and supplier, and displays everything in a live comparison dashboard.
