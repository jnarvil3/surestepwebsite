Automated Lead Prospecting That Replaced 20+ Hours of Manual Research

A B2B company needed to build targeted prospect lists for outreach, but their team was spending days manually searching Google Maps, visiting websites, and copying contact info into spreadsheets.

The Solution

An End-to-End Lead Enrichment Pipeline

We built an end-to-end pipeline that searches Google Maps for businesses matching target criteria, crawls their websites to extract emails and contact details, scores and ranks each lead, and delivers a clean, enriched list ready for outreach.
