An Automated Lead Qualifier That Turns a Signup List Into a Prioritized Call Sheet in Minutes

A startup founder was manually researching every inbound lead - Googling names, scanning LinkedIn, reading company pages - spending 15-20 minutes per person just to decide if they were worth a call. With dozens of signups a week, the best leads often went cold before anyone reached out.

The Solution

Automated lead qualification that scores, ranks, and prioritizes in seconds

We built an automated qualification pipeline that takes an email address, searches across four web APIs in parallel, and uses AI to score each lead on relevant dimensions like track record, ambition, and industry fit. The result: a ranked, tiered list with enough context to sound informed on the first call - delivered in seconds, not hours.
