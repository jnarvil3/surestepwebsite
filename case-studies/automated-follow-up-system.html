An Automated Follow-Up System That Recovered 35% of Dropped Leads

A B2B services firm was losing deals because follow-ups fell through the cracks. Reps were juggling dozens of prospects manually — some got three emails, others got none. There was no system, just good intentions and sticky notes.

What We Built

A sales follow-up automation engine triggered by prospect behavior

We built an automated follow-up engine that triggers personalized sequences based on prospect behavior — opened a proposal but didn't reply, visited the pricing page, or went quiet after a call. Each touchpoint is timed, tracked, and escalated if needed.

The system replaced the chaos of manual tracking with a structured, behavior-driven sequence. Every lead now gets timely, relevant follow-up without reps having to remember who needs what. Managers get full visibility into pipeline activity, and nothing slips through the cracks.
