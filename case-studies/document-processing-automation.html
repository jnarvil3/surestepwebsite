Automated Document Processing That Eliminated 25 Hours of Manual Sorting Per Week

An accounting firm received hundreds of documents weekly — invoices, contracts, delivery notes, and receipts — all arriving by email in different formats. A junior team member spent most of their week opening, reading, classifying, and filing each one by hand.

The solution

AI-powered document classification and automated filing

We built a pipeline that automatically extracts text from incoming documents (including scanned PDFs and photos), classifies them by type, pulls out key fields like amounts, dates, and vendor names, and routes them to the right folder — flagging anything uncertain for human review.
